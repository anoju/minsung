Product Overview

MSSP Series Certified Metallic Stopping (Blanking) Plugs provide
a method of sealing unused entries in Ex equipment.

Application : Stopping plug

Manufacturer Minsung Precision Co.,Ltd
Design Specification ISO 965-1/965-3, ANSI/ASME B1.20.1
Compliance Standard IEC 60079-0:2017
IEC 60079-1:2014
IEC 60079-7:2015
IEC 60079-31:2013

Code of Protection Ex db ⅡC Gb, Ex eb ⅡC Gb,Ex tb ⅢC Db
Ambient Temperature -20℃~110℃
Operating Temperature -20℃~140℃ (O-Ring, Washer)
Ingress Protection IP66 / IP67
Plug Material Nickel Plated Brass, Stainless Steel(316L)
Gasket (washer, O-Ring) PTFE-Teflon (washer), FKM (O-Ring)
Accessories (optional) Lock Nut
  • Metric entry threads comply with ISO 965-1 and ISO 965-3 with a 6g tolerance as required by IEC 60079-1:2014.
    The MSSP standard metric thread pitch is 1.5mm for threads up to M75, and 2.0mm from M90 and above.
  • NPT threads are in accordance with ASME/ANSI B1.20.1
